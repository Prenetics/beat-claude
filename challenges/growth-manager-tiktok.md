# Challenge: Growth Manager — TikTok Shop

**Role:** Growth Manager (TikTok Shop)
**Brand:** IM8
**Difficulty:** Hard
**Time:** 1–2 hours
**Max Length:** 4 pages

---

## Background

IM8 is a premium health & supplements brand co-founded by David Beckham. We sell direct-to-consumer across multiple channels, and TikTok Shop is one of our most important growth frontiers.

We currently have:
- A TikTok Shop storefront with ~20 SKUs (supplements, bundles)
- A small affiliate network (~15 active creators)
- Monthly TikTok Shop GMV of ~$25K
- 1 LIVE shopping event per week (inconsistent results)
- Average order value: $45
- TikTok Shop conversion rate: 1.8%

## The Challenge

**You have 90 days to 5x our TikTok Shop GMV from $25K/month to $125K/month.**

Build us a detailed 90-day growth plan that covers:

1. **Affiliate Programme Scale-Up** — How do you go from 15 to 100+ active affiliates? What's the tiered structure, commission model, and recruitment strategy?

2. **LIVE Commerce Strategy** — How do you turn our 1x/week LIVE into a revenue engine? Frequency, talent, format, promotional mechanics.

3. **Content & Conversion** — What content formats will drive the most shop discovery and conversion? How do you brief creators without killing authenticity?

4. **Paid Amplification** — How do you use Spark Ads and paid media to amplify top-performing organic/affiliate content? What's your budget allocation framework?

5. **Measurement & Optimisation** — What KPIs do you track weekly? What does your reporting cadence look like? How do you make fast decisions on what's working and what's not?

## What We're NOT Looking For

- Generic "build a content calendar" advice
- Copy-paste TikTok best practices from a blog post
- A strategy that could apply to any brand on any platform

## What WILL Impress Us

- Specific numbers, timelines, and named tactics
- Understanding of TikTok Shop's algorithm and commerce mechanics
- Creative affiliate incentive structures that actually drive behaviour
- Evidence you've done this before (or deeply understand the space)
- Ideas that make Claude's answer look basic
