# Beat Claude Challenge: Ambassador & Partnerships Manager

**IM8 Health | Prenetics (NASDAQ: PRE)**
*Co-founded by David Beckham*

---

## The Challenge

We gave Claude this exact brief. It produced a well-researched, structured, strategically sound response.

**Your job is to beat it.**

Claude can map competitive landscapes, build partnership tiers, and draft activation frameworks all day long. What it can't do is read a room in a meeting with an athlete's management team, feel when a creator is about to blow up before the data shows it, or know which partnerships will actually move product versus just look good on Instagram.

This challenge is designed to find people who can.

---

**Current public ambassadors:**
- **David Beckham** — Co-founder and global face of the brand
- **Aryna Sabalenka** — World #1 tennis player
- **Oliver Bearman** — F1 driver (Haas)

IM8 operates across the **US, UK, Hong Kong, and APAC** markets.

---

## The Role

You will own IM8's ambassador program and strategic partnerships end-to-end. That means:

- **Athlete & celebrity ambassador relationships** — managing existing, sourcing new
- **Creator & influencer partnerships** — identifying, negotiating, activating
- **Brand collaborations** — strategic partnerships that extend IM8's reach
- **Affiliate programs** — building the ecosystem that drives revenue, not just awareness
- **Multi-market execution** — US, UK, HK, and APAC. Different markets, different playbooks.

You're building the ecosystem that drives both **brand equity AND revenue**. Awareness without conversion is a vanity project. Conversion without brand is a race to the bottom. We need someone who understands both.

## Part 1: Partnership Landscape Audit (35%)

### What We Need From You

**A. Map IM8's Current Partnership Ecosystem**

Using publicly available information (im8health.com, IM8 social channels, ambassador social posts, press coverage), map out IM8's current partnership landscape:

- Who are the current ambassadors and what role does each play in the brand?
- What content are they producing? What channels? What cadence?
- How are these partnerships being activated — pure brand awareness, content creation, direct commerce, affiliate, or a combination?
- What's the apparent partnership structure (co-founder vs. paid ambassador vs. campaign-based)?

**B. Competitive Analysis**

Compare IM8's partnership strategy to at least **4 competitors** in the premium supplement / wellness space. Required comparisons:

| Brand | Key Ambassadors/Partners | Partnership Model | Content Strategy | Commerce Integration | What IM8 Can Learn |
|-------|--------------------------|-------------------|------------------|---------------------|--------------------|

Suggested competitors (choose at least 4):
- **AG1 (Athletic Greens)** — the podcast/creator juggernaut
- **Bloom Nutrition** — TikTok-native, creator-first
- **Huel** — performance/lifestyle positioning
- **Momentous** — Huberman-affiliated, science-led
- **LMNT** — creator-driven, community-first
- **Seed** — premium health, science-backed
- Others you think are relevant

**C. Gap Analysis**

Based on your audit:
- What is IM8 doing well that competitors aren't?
- What are competitors doing that IM8 is missing?
- Where is the biggest untapped opportunity for IM8's partnership strategy?
- What's the single highest-ROI partnership move IM8 could make in the next 90 days?

Be specific. "IM8 should work with more creators" is not an insight. Tell us who, why, what the deal looks like, and what the expected impact is.

---

## Part 2: 6-Month Partnership Plan (35%)

### What We Need From You

Design IM8's partnership roadmap for the next 6 months. This is open-ended by design — show us how you think about building a partnership ecosystem for a premium brand.

**Your plan should cover:**

- **Partnership Tiers** — How would you structure IM8's partnership programme? What tiers exist, what does each look like, and how are they compensated?

- **Target List** — Who would you go after? Name specific people and brands. Tell us why they're a fit and how you'd pitch them.

- **Activation** — For your top picks, how do you actually bring the partnership to life? What content gets created, where does it live, and how does it drive subscriptions?

- **Market Strategy** — IM8 operates in US, UK, HK, and APAC. How does the partnership approach differ by market?

- **Measurement** — How do you prove partnerships are working? How do you decide when to double down or walk away?

We're deliberately not prescribing the format or level of detail for each section. How you choose to structure and prioritise this plan tells us as much as the content itself.

## Part 3: AI Integration (20%)

How would you use AI to build and scale IM8's partnership program?

We want **specific, implementable ideas** — not "use AI to analyze data."

**Address the following:**

**A. Partner Discovery & Sourcing**
- How would you use AI tools to identify potential partners at scale?
- What data signals matter? How would you score and rank prospects?
- How do you find the next Aryna Sabalenka before AG1 does?

**B. Content & Performance Analysis**
- How would you use AI to monitor ambassador content performance across platforms?
- What does an AI-powered partnership dashboard look like?
- How do you use AI to identify which content formats, hooks, and CTAs drive subscriptions vs. just engagement?

**C. Outreach & Relationship Management**
- How would you use AI in the outreach process without making it feel robotic?
- How do you personalize at scale while maintaining IM8's premium positioning?

**D. Competitive Intelligence**
- How would you use AI to monitor competitor partnership moves in real-time?
- What would an automated competitive intelligence briefing look like?

**E. Content Creation & Repurposing**
- How would you use AI to help ambassadors create better content, faster?
- How do you repurpose one ambassador video into 20 pieces of content across platforms?

For each idea: what tool or approach, what's the expected impact, and what does implementation look like in practice?

## Part 4: Presentation (10%)

**Format & Communication Quality**

Your submission itself is a deliverable. We're evaluating:
- Is it clear, structured, and easy to follow?
- Can a non-marketing executive (e.g., CFO, CEO) understand your plan and its expected ROI?
- Does it feel like a plan from someone who's done this before, or someone who's researched what it looks like?
- Visual aids, tables, and frameworks are encouraged — but substance over style.

---

## What We're Evaluating

- **Market instinct** — Do you understand the premium wellness space and where IM8 sits in it?
- **Commercial thinking** — Every partnership should connect to revenue. Brand awareness is necessary but not sufficient.
- **Specificity** — Named targets, deal ranges, activation details. Not "we should partner with athletes."
- **Multi-market awareness** — The US, UK, and APAC are different worlds. Show you know that.
- **Taste** — IM8 is co-founded by David Beckham. The partnership ecosystem needs to match that caliber. Who belongs and who doesn't?
- **Operational rigor** — Can you actually execute this plan, or is it a strategy deck that never becomes real?

## What We're NOT Looking For

- A list of 50 influencers pulled from a database with no rationale
- Generic "partnership strategy frameworks" without IM8-specific application
- Ignoring the commercial side — we're not building a brand book, we're driving subscriptions
- Copy-paste competitive analysis that doesn't lead to actionable insights
- Submissions that read like they were AI-generated and submitted without adding real industry knowledge, relationships context, or original thinking
- Playing it safe — we want bold, defensible recommendations, not obvious ones
