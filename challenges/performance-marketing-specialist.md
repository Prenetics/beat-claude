## Part 1: Paid Media Audit (35%)

### What to do:

Conduct a comprehensive audit of IM8's current paid media presence using only publicly available information. Your audit should cover:

### Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

- **Ad Library Analysis:** Go to Meta Ad Library and pull every active IM8 ad. Document:
  - Total number of active ads
  - Ad formats in use (video, static, carousel, collection)
  - Messaging angles and value propositions used
  - CTAs and destination URLs
  - Creative freshness — estimated age of ads, frequency of new creatives
  - Geographic targeting (which countries are ads running in?)
- **Creative Assessment:** Which ads are likely performing best and why? Rate the overall creative quality and variety. Is IM8 testing enough variants?
- **Landing Page Analysis:** Click through to every unique landing page destination. Evaluate:
  - Message match between ad and landing page
  - Page load speed and mobile experience
  - Conversion path clarity — how many clicks from ad to purchase?
  - Trust signals present (NSF certification, RCT, Beckham, reviews)
  - Subscription vs. one-time purchase positioning

### Google / Search

- **Google Ads Transparency Center:** Search for IM8 Health ads. Document any active search, display, or YouTube ads.
- **SEO Snapshot:** Using free tools (Google search, Ubersuggest free tier, or similar), assess:
  - What branded and non-branded terms does IM8 appear to rank for?
  - Is there evidence of brand bidding by competitors?
  - What does the SERP look like for key terms like "daily vitamin subscription," "best daily vitamins," "AG1 alternative"?
- **Shopping & Product Listings:** Is IM8 running Google Shopping ads? How does the product appear in Google Shopping results?

### TikTok & YouTube

- **TikTok Ads:** Check TikTok Creative Center / Ad Library for IM8 ads. Document any paid content.
- **YouTube Ads:** Note any pre-roll, in-stream, or Shorts ads you can find (Google Ads Transparency Center or direct observation).

### Competitive Paid Media Comparison

Pull ad library data for 2–3 key competitors (AG1, Ritual, Momentous, or others you deem relevant) and compare:

- Creative volume and variety
- Messaging angles (science-led, lifestyle, founder-led, discount-driven)
- Landing page experience and conversion path
- Estimated media mix (which channels do they appear most active on?)

### Gap Analysis:

Synthesize your audit into a clear assessment. Identify the **top 5–7 paid media opportunities** for IM8, ranked by potential impact on customer acquisition cost (CAC) and return on ad spend (ROAS).

### What we want to see:

- Screenshots and specific references — not generalized observations
- A performance marketer's lens: you're evaluating ads for effectiveness, not aesthetics
- Evidence of rigorous methodology — you checked every available source
- Actionable insights that connect to business metrics

## Part 2: Full-Funnel Paid Acquisition Strategy (35%)

### What to do:

Design a comprehensive paid acquisition strategy for IM8 Health. This should be a strategy you could present to the VP of Marketing and start executing in your first month.

### Your strategy must include:

#### Channel Strategy & Media Mix

- **Channel Prioritization:** Rank the channels IM8 should invest in and why. For each channel, provide:
  - Role in the funnel (awareness, consideration, conversion, retention)
  - Expected efficiency (relative CPA/ROAS)
  - Content/creative requirements
  - Audience targeting approach
- **Recommended Channels:** At minimum, address Meta Ads, Google Ads (Search + Shopping + YouTube), TikTok Ads. Optionally include: programmatic/display, podcast ads, influencer-paid hybrid, affiliate, or emerging channels.
- **Media Mix Recommendation:** Given a hypothetical monthly budget of $100K, how would you allocate across channels? Show percentages and rationale.

#### Campaign Architecture

For your top 2 channels, design the full campaign structure:

- **Campaign/Ad Set Structure:** How do you organize campaigns? What's your naming convention? How do you structure audiences?
- **Audience Strategy:** Detail your targeting approach at each funnel stage:
  - Prospecting: What cold audiences? Lookalikes based on what seed? Interest-based? Broad?
  - Retargeting: What segments? What windows? What exclusions?
  - Retention/Winback: How do you re-engage lapsed subscribers?
- **Bidding & Budget Strategy:** What bid strategies for each campaign type? How do you manage budget across campaigns? What's your approach to scaling winners?
- **Creative Testing Framework:**
  - How many creative variants per ad set?
  - What variables do you test (hook, format, messaging, CTA, landing page)?
  - What's your statistical significance threshold?
  - How do you kill losers and scale winners? Provide specific criteria.

#### Unit Economics & Targets

- **Target Metrics:** Define target KPIs for each channel:
  - CPA (cost per acquisition)
  - ROAS (return on ad spend)
  - CTR, CPC, CVR benchmarks
  - CAC payback period
- **LTV Consideration:** IM8 is a subscription product. How does subscription LTV change your willingness to pay for acquisition? What blended CAC target makes sense for a premium supplement subscription?
- **Scaling Framework:** At what metrics do you increase spend? When do you pull back? What's your approach to scaling from $100K/month to $250K/month to $500K/month?

#### Landing Page & Conversion Strategy

- **Landing Page Recommendations:** Based on your audit, what changes would you make to IM8's landing pages to improve conversion rate?
- **Conversion Path Optimization:** How many steps from ad click to purchase? Where are the likely drop-off points?
- **A/B Testing Roadmap:** What are the first 3–5 landing page tests you'd run, ranked by expected impact?

### Consider these IM8-specific angles:

- **Premium pricing** in a market where AG1 already faces "expensive" objections — how do you position value in ads?
- **Subscription model** means first-order CPA matters less than subscription retention — how does this affect your bidding and targeting strategy?
- **David Beckham** as co-founder is a unique asset — how do you deploy celebrity credibility in paid media without it feeling like a generic endorsement?
- **NSF Certified for Sport + RCT** are trust signals that most competitors can't match — where in the funnel do these convert best?
- **"92 nutrients in one serving"** — how do you make this rational benefit emotionally compelling in a 3-second ad hook?
- **Multi-market** (IM8 appears to operate in multiple markets) — how does your strategy account for geographic differences?
