## The Challenge

You are the newly hired Video Editor at IM8 Health. Your first assignment: audit what's currently live, pitch new video concepts, demonstrate your editing ability, and show us the AI-powered workflow you'd bring to the team.

## Part 1: Video Content Audit (30%)

### What to do:

Conduct a thorough audit of IM8's existing video content across TikTok, Instagram (Reels + Stories highlights), and YouTube. Your audit should cover:

- **Content Inventory:** Categorize the types of video content IM8 currently publishes (e.g., founder-led, UGC-style, product demos, ambassador content, educational, lifestyle). What's the current content mix?
- **Performance Signals:** Using publicly visible metrics (views, likes, comments, shares), identify IM8's top-performing videos and worst-performing videos. What patterns emerge?
- **Hook Analysis:** Break down the first 3 seconds of IM8's top 5 videos. What hooks are being used? Are they effective? What's the scroll-stop rate likely to be?
- **Editing Style Assessment:** Evaluate the current editing style — pacing, transitions, text overlays, sound design, color grading, aspect ratios. Is it consistent? Is it platform-native?
- **Competitor Comparison:** Review video content from 2–3 competitors (e.g., AG1, Ritual, Momentous, LMNT) and compare production quality, content strategy, and engagement.
- **Gap Analysis & Recommendations:** What types of content is IM8 missing? What formats should they test? What's the single biggest editing improvement that would lift performance?

### What we want to see:

- Specific video references (links or screenshots) — not vague generalizations
- A trained eye for what makes content perform on short-form platforms
- Constructive criticism that's actionable, not just "the editing could be better"
- Understanding of how editing decisions impact retention and engagement metrics

## Part 2: Video Ad Concepts & Storyboards (30%)

### What to do:

Create **3 original video ad concepts** for IM8 Health. Each concept should be designed for paid social (TikTok Ads, Meta Ads, YouTube Shorts) and organic distribution.

### For each concept, provide:

- **Concept Title & Format:** (e.g., "The Morning Ritual" — UGC-style testimonial, 30s)
- **Target Audience:** Who is this video for? What stage of the funnel?
- **Platform:** Primary platform and any platform-specific considerations
- **Hook (0–3 seconds):** The exact opening frame/moment. This is the most important part — what stops the scroll?
- **Structure & Shot List:** Beat-by-beat breakdown of the video. What's on screen, what's the voiceover/text overlay, what's the music/sound?
- **CTA:** How does the video end? What action does the viewer take?
- **Editing Notes:** Pacing, transitions, text style, color treatment, sound design direction
- **Why This Works:** Strategic rationale — why will this concept perform? What insight or trend does it tap into?

### Your 3 concepts should demonstrate range:

- **Concept 1:** A performance-focused ad designed to drive conversions (bottom of funnel)
- **Concept 2:** A brand/awareness play designed for reach and engagement (top of funnel)
- **Concept 3:** A trend-native or culturally relevant piece designed for organic virality

### Consider these IM8-specific angles:

- David Beckham as co-founder is a massive asset — how do you leverage celebrity without making it feel like a generic endorsement?
- "92 nutrients in one serving" is a powerful rational message — how do you make that visual and visceral?
- NSF Certified for Sport signals trust for athletes — can this be a hook?
- The 12-week RCT is a unique differentiator in the supplement space — how do you make clinical evidence feel compelling on TikTok?
- Aryna Sabalenka and Oliver Bearman represent elite sport across tennis and F1 — how do you use this?

## Part 3: Spec Edit (20%)

### What to do:

Demonstrate your editing ability by creating a short-form video edit (15–45 seconds) using **publicly available IM8 assets only**.

### Option A: Spec Edit from Public Assets

Using footage, images, and audio sourced from IM8's public channels (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, website), create an original edit that could serve as a paid ad or organic post. You may also use:

- Royalty-free music/sound effects
- Stock footage (if clearly relevant)
- Screen recordings of the IM8 website or social channels
- Text overlays, motion graphics, and your own design elements

### Option B: Reference Clip Re-Edit

Choose one of IM8's existing videos from any platform and re-edit it. Show us what you would change — pacing, structure, hooks, text, sound design, color — and explain why in a brief written note alongside the edit.

### What we want to see:

- Platform-native editing (this should feel like it belongs on TikTok/Reels, not a TV commercial)
- Strong hook in the first 1–3 seconds
- Clean execution — text timing, audio sync, transitions, pacing
- A sense of IM8's premium brand identity translated into short-form
- Your creative instinct and personal editing style

### Deliverable:

- MP4 file, vertical (9:16), optimized for mobile
- Brief written note (3–5 sentences) explaining your creative decisions

## Part 4: AI Workflow (20%)

### What to do:

Show us exactly how AI fits into your video editing and content creation pipeline. This is not a wishlist — we want to see your actual workflow.

### We want to see:

- **AI Tools You Actually Use:** What's in your daily toolkit? (e.g., Runway, Descript, CapCut AI features, ElevenLabs, Midjourney, ChatGPT for scripts, etc.) Be specific about what each tool does for you.
- **Script & Concept Generation:** Show your actual prompts for generating video scripts, hooks, or ad concepts. Include the AI output and explain how you refine it.
- **Editing Acceleration:** How does AI speed up your editing workflow? Auto-captions, background removal, audio enhancement, B-roll generation, color matching — show specific examples.
- **Thumbnail & Visual Asset Creation:** Do you use AI for thumbnail generation, text overlay design, or visual concepting? Show examples.
- **Prompt Library:** Share 3–5 of your best prompts for video content tasks, with context on when and how you use each one.
- **Volume Play:** How does AI help you produce more content without sacrificing quality? What's your throughput with AI vs. without?

### Important:

We're building an AI-powered content engine. We need editors who don't just know how to use AI tools — they've already integrated them into how they work. Show us prompts. Show us outputs. Show us before/after. If you're not using AI in your editing workflow yet, this role probably isn't the right fit.
